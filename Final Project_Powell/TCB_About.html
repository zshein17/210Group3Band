Learn a little more about us here
The Tidewater Concert Band has been providing entertainment to Hampton Roads since 1971. The band is currently under the direction of Chris Serrano and Associate Conductor Josiah Wilson. We cover styles from Big Band, Sousa marches, Holiday, to Broadway, Swing and Blues. We provide music for festivals, local parades, graduation ceremonies and art shows, as well as retirement homes. We are always looking for new opportunities to entertain Tidewater.
