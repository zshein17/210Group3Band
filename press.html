Press Kit

The Tidewater Concert Band has provided musical entertainment ranging from Sousa Style Marches, Big Band, Swing, Blues, Broadway Musicals, etc. in the Hampton Roads area since 1971. Currently under the direction of Maestro Chris Serrano and Associate Conductor Josiah Wilson, the band is currently made up of volunteer musicians from every walks of life including local school band directors, band students, former and current military musicians... to form a fully-instrumented top-notch professional performing organization.

The band has gone through many changes, phases and musicians throughout its long history but still continues today to provide a high quality of music and service to the Hampton Roads community. The band provides music for graduation ceremonies, as well as, local parades, family celebrations, art shows, festivals, retirement-nursing homes and concert series. The group always welcomes new performance opportunities in the 7 cities. Take this time and bring this wonderful, entertaining musical group to your venue or next event!!

We play the following music:
  • Patriotic
  • Traditional
  • Holidy
